Excel Image Zoomer

A simple add-on to instantly enlarge images from your spreadsheet cells.

Features

  • Click on any cell image to view it in full size
  • Clean and intuitive UI
  • Works with Google Sheets (Excel support coming soon)
  • Supports multiple images per sheet

Installation

  1. Go to the Google Workspace Marketplace.
  2. Search for Excel Image Zoomer.
  3. Click Install and follow the instructions.
  4. Open Google Sheets and activate the add-on from the Extensions menu.

How to Use

  1. Insert images into your spreadsheet cells.
  2. Click an image thumbnail to enlarge it.
  3. Click outside the image or the close button to exit zoom mode.
